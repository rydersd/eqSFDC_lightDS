📚 Chapter 1: "We Are the Knights Who Say... Ni!"
The Knights Who Say Ni Ltd began as a humble shrubbery procurement operation in the forests of ancient Britain. Their Head Knight demanded a very specific thing from every traveler who passed through: "A shrubbery! One that looks nice. And not too expensive." Over time, the Knights diversified into professional services — specifically, helping organizations find exactly the right thing they didn't know they needed. Their latest venture: migrating their entire shrubbery catalog to a cloud-hosted platform, which naturally requires colocation services. The Black Knight, their famously persistent Security Director ("It's just a flesh wound!"), manages the relationship. He has never, not once, allowed a vendor to pass without a fight.
