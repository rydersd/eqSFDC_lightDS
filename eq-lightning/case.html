Chapter 5: "You Must Cut Down the Mightiest Tree in the Forest With... a Herring!"
Roger the Shrubber, VP of Shrubbery Operations at the Knights Who Say Ni, attempted to install a herring in Rack B at the LD5 facility. The Head Knight had insisted that the network topology be "herring-shaped" and Roger interpreted this literally. He arrived at the data center with an actual herring and attempted to mount it between the two Fabric ports. The NOC team intercepted the situation, but not before Roger had unplugged a fiber patch cable to "make room for the herring." This caused a brief connectivity blip on the Ni! API (47 seconds of downtime, 12,000 failed "Ni!" requests). The Bridge Keeper — our senior support engineer — is now working the case. He has a policy of asking three questions before allowing any resolution to pass.

Design discussion: The Case record is about speed to resolution. Left = related records, knowledge articles, and assets. Center = case details, SLA timers, and resolution. Right = activity timeline with Chatter and Agentforce tabs.
