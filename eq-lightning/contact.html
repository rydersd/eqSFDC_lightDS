Chapter 2: "Brave Sir Robin Ran Away..."
Sir Robin is the Knights Who Say Ni's primary procurement contact. His minstrels follow him everywhere, composing ballads about his negotiations: "Bravely bold Sir Robin rode forth from Camelot... when danger reared its ugly head, he bravely turned his tail and fled." Despite this reputation, Robin actually closes more deals than anyone — because he's the first to suggest compromise. Before the redesign, Robin had to check Salesforce for the account, Siebel for the order history, then email for the latest minstrel performance reviews. He called it "the retreat from context" and it took him ~14 minutes per prep. He would have run away sooner, but he couldn't find the exit button.

Design discussion: The Contact record answers: Who is this person, what do they care about, and what have we done with them recently? Left column = related records and account context. Center = identity and role details. Right = engagement history with tabbed Activity, Chatter, and Agentforce. Robin needs to see all of this without swivel-chairing between three systems.
