Description

This 36-month contract covers four (4) IBX cabinets at the London LD5 data center with full Equinix Fabric connectivity (2x 10Gbps ports), SmartHands hands-on support services (10 hours per month), and comprehensive SLA coverage with 99.999% uptime guarantee.
