Project Context

Equinix is standardizing its Salesforce Lightning experience across a global sales organization. The current state includes 800+ custom objects (consolidated to ~160), pages with 175-225 field sections and 190+ action buttons, and a swivel-chair workflow between Salesforce, Siebel, and email.

This prototype defines the target assembly pattern: how standard Salesforce Lightning components should be composed into consistent, persona-aware record pages. The three-column layout (Details | Activity | Related) serves as the foundation, with Agentforce touchpoints woven in where they reduce manual work and surface intelligence.

The Monty Python data story keeps stakeholder conversations about required content, layout decisions, and field groupings engaging and low-stakes â€” so people focus on the structure, not the data.
