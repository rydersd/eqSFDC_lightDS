The Knights Who Say Ni placed a third order — an expansion into DC3 for their planned "Shrubbery Replication Service." But something went wrong. During credit review, the finance team discovered that the Knights had taken out a second shrubbery loan that pushed their debt-to-equity ratio past the threshold. Galahad insists his credit is pure, but the system disagrees. The order has been On Hold for 11 days now, and the pricing team flagged that the original quotes were generated against the Q4 2025 rate card — which expired three weeks ago. So even when the credit hold lifts, the prices need refreshing before the order can advance. This is exactly the kind of compound exception that gets buried in the current system — two separate issues on the same order, neither visible from the list view unless you drill in.
