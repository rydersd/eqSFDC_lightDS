Assembly Decisions

  • Left column (Related): Order Line Items with provisioning status, Related Cases linked to this order, and Shrubbery Inventory tracking. Compact related lists surface order fulfillment details at a glance.
  • Center column (Details): Order information (number, status, dates), shipping and billing addresses, and key terms. Path component shows order lifecycle: Draft, Activated, Provisioning, Complete.
  • Right column (Activity): Tabbed interface with Order Activity timeline (provisioning events, invoice checks, incidents), Chatter for order team discussion, and Agentforce tab with Auto-Populate from Quote and Price Validation cards.

Open Questions
